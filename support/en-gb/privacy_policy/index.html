4. Creating a Profile Card

a) Data Processing when Creating a Profile Card
When you create a Profile Card and provide data in your Profile Card (name, player level and ID, Alliance Goal and personalised text in the comment section), the data is processed on your browser through Javascript(canvas) without collection or processing by Nintendo.
The browser will delete the data provided for creating your Profile Card when you close the page.

b) Sharing your Profile Card on Twitter
If you wish to do so, you can share your Profile Card on your Twitter Account by using the “Tweet Your Profile Card!” button displayed next to the download button.
The “Tweet Your Profile Card!” button is offered by Twitter Inc., 1355 Market St, Suite 900, San Francisco, CA 94103 (“Twitter”). By using the “Tweet Your Profile Card!” button, your Profile Card image file created in your browser and any text you choose to input into the Tweet field are temporarily sent to Nintendo’s server and to a cookie placed in this regard, are shared with your Twitter account, and are made available on your Twitter account to other users.
Nintendo will delete the data provided for posting your Profile Card on Twitter and the cookie placed in this regard as soon as you either post on Twitter or close the Profile Card webpage. If you do not post your Profile Card on Twitter, yet do stay on the page, the provided data and the placed Cookie will be deleted automatically within one day at the latest, after it has been created.
If you are located in a country of the European Union, this processing is based on your consent (Art. 6 para. 1 (a) GDPR).
Please note that Nintendo as provider of the website does not have any further knowledge of the content of the data transmitted to or used by Twitter. You can find further information on this in Twitter’s privacy policy at https://twitter.com/privacy.
You can change your data protection settings on Twitter in the account settings at https://twitter.com/account/settings.
